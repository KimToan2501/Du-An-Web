Kit Cat Fillet ‘O’ Flakes is a premium and complete cat food specially crafted for adult cats over 1 year old. Enriched with Omega-3, Omega-6, taurine, prebiotic vitamins, and free from pork or lard, it supports your cat’s overall health and well-being.
